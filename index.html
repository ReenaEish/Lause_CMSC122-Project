How to Play

Objective:

Arrange the numbered tiles in sequential order from 1 to 15, with the empty slot positioned at the bottom-right corner of the grid.

Game Controls:

  • Click or tap on a tile next to the empty slot to slide it into that position.
  • Alternatively, you can use the keyboard arrow keys (or W, A, S, D keys) to move tiles in the desired direction.

Hints and Options:

  • Hint: Click the "Hint" button for a suggestion on your next move.
  • Give Up: Click "Give Up" to view the solution steps and see the puzzle solved.
  • Restart: Click "Restart" to reset the current puzzle arrangement.

Goodluck, and enjoy solving the NumSlider!
